What We Do
Ideal Schools

We are a group of threesome women who had difficulties in finding schools for their children hence did not want the same for the community in general to endure the common pain.Ideal Schools was founded in 2016 and we only dealt with schools in Nairobi but now we are extended to Mombasa and Kisumu.Ideal schools has faced a great number of success stories as featured in our testimonials.We guide and educate parents on factors to consider while choosing the best schools for their children,we also offer school visit tours so that the parents can attest that what they were shown is what they see for seeing is beliving. You have a traumatized experience when in school Or does your child? Then come join our guide and counselling classes and get the right help for we believe that psychological health is the key to greatness.
